#  Apple Stock Time Series Analysis Report

## 1. Introduction
This project performs time series analysis on Apple Inc. (AAPL) stock data from 2020 to 2025. The objective is to analyze historical stock price movements and identify trends using data visualization techniques.

---

## 2. Objective
- To analyze Apple stock price trends over time  
- To visualize stock price fluctuations  
- To apply moving average for smoothing data  
- To understand long-term market behavior  

---

## 3. Dataset Description
The dataset was obtained using the `yfinance` library in Python.

- Stock Symbol: AAPL  
- Source: Yahoo Finance (via yfinance)  
- Time Period: 2020 - 2025  
- Feature Used: Closing Price  

---

## 4. Tools & Technologies
- Python  
- Pandas  
- NumPy  
- Matplotlib  
- yfinance  

---

## 5. Methodology
1. Data collection using yfinance  
2. Data preprocessing and cleaning  
3. Selection of relevant columns (Date, Close)  
4. Time series visualization  
5. Calculation of 50-day moving average  
6. Trend analysis and interpretation  

---

## 6. Analysis & Results
- Apple stock shows an overall upward trend over the selected period  
- Short-term fluctuations indicate market volatility  
- Moving average helps smooth the trend and highlight long-term movement  
- Stock prices tend to fluctuate but generally follow growth direction  

---

## 7. Conclusion
Time series analysis helps in understanding stock market behavior and identifying trends over time. This project demonstrates how historical data can be used for meaningful insights and financial analysis.
